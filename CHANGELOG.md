# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-08

### Added
- Nuclear Bomb item - massive explosion with fire and radiation
- Parks mob - the ultimate pet that kills everything
- Custom sounds for nuclear explosion
- Custom particle effects
- Taming mechanics for Parks

### Features
- Nuclear explosions destroy blocks in 50 block radius
- Radiation damage extends to 80 blocks
- Parks has 500 health and does 1000 damage
- Parks is immune to fire, explosions, and magic
- Kill counter tracks Parks's victims
